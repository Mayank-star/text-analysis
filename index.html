What is character count ?

Although some think that character count is the total number of letters in a text, it is the total count of all characters, including spaces. It is mostly needed when a text or a document must stay within certain numbers of characters. For instance, Facebook posts often have specific character limits that you should be aware of. Additionally, some translators use character count without spaces to determine the price of a translation job.
